SpaceBattle

AI beats space captain.

Instructions
  1. Select your space ships from left-hand
  2. Place them on your map
  3. Shoot on cells of enemy's map
    to find and destroy all enemy ships
  4. AI will fire on yours
    immediately after you fire on its
Legend
  • blue - space (empty)
  • grey - undamaged space ship
  • white - empty with a hit in it (missed shot)
  • red - damaged ship (hit shot)
  • black - destroyed
